Six Core Services

Six Pillars of Student Success

01

Dream School Planning

We begin by building a deep understanding of your child — their academic strengths, personal interests, extracurricular achievements, and long-term career goals. From this foundation, we identify the optimal college list: a carefully balanced mix of reach, match, and safety schools.

  • Personalized university list development
  • Major and career pathway exploration
  • School research and campus culture matching
  • Application timeline planning
02

Standout Applications

A strong GPA and test scores are just the starting point. What truly differentiates successful applicants is how they tell their story. Our advisors help each student uncover their most compelling narrative and translate it into an application that stands out.

  • Common App and UC Application strategy
  • Activities list optimization
  • Awards and honors presentation
  • Application review and quality control
03

Essay & Interview Coaching

The personal statement and supplemental essays are your child's single greatest opportunity to speak directly to admissions officers. Our essay coaching process helps students find their authentic voice, develop a compelling theme, and craft essays that are memorable and genuine.

  • Personal statement brainstorming and drafting
  • Supplemental essay coaching for all schools
  • Multiple revision rounds with expert feedback
  • Mock interview preparation and practice
04

Academic & Activities Strategy

Top universities look for students who have challenged themselves academically and pursued their interests with genuine depth. We help families plan course selection, choose meaningful extracurriculars, and build a cohesive student profile starting as early as 9th grade.

  • Course selection and AP/IB planning
  • Extracurricular portfolio development
  • Community service and leadership strategy
  • SAT/ACT test prep guidance and scheduling
05

Summer & Research Programs

Meaningful summer experiences — research internships, university programs, and academic enrichment — can dramatically strengthen a student's application. Through our network of partners at UC Berkeley, UCLA, and Stanford, we provide exclusive access to opportunities that are otherwise difficult to find.

  • UC Berkeley and UCLA program placement
  • Stanford pre-collegiate program access
  • Research internship connections
  • Program application support
06

Competitions & Hackathons

Admissions officers are impressed by students who have tested their skills in competitive, real-world settings. We connect our students with a curated selection of academic competitions, science fairs, and technology hackathons that align with their interests and add meaningful distinction.

  • Academic competition identification
  • Science fair and research competition prep
  • Technology hackathon connections
  • Award documentation for applications
